My Favorite Hobby

About

Reading is one of my favorite hobbies because I enjoy getting into the characters mindset and thoughts. It is nice to jump into the reality of the book when I'm reading. For example, books like Harry Potter or Hunger Games have such good plots and realities. When I'm reading it is fun to just sit back and let my mind take a leap. I normally read during the night time or when I am done with an assignment at school because most teachers encourage reading. Reading is also just a safe space to get away from anything stressful in your life.

My Favorite Books

My favorite book is Harry Potter and the Sorcerer's Stone, because the plot is just so good! I mean, Harry is a wizard! The whole book itself just interests me in general from the front to the back.
